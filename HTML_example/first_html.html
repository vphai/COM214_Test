Alicia Keys' New Song

Alicia Keys has unveiled new song "That's What's Up" along with an accompanying video on her birthday on Wednesday. The inspirational tune samples Kanye West's "Low Lights," which hails from The Life of Pablo. The singer posted the clip on Facebook, where she revealed the video was a birthday song and wrote, "I'm so thankful!" The low-key footage is shot home movie-style and follows the singer as she's lounging in a bathrobe and later singing in a bathtub. [Rolling Stone-1/30/17]
